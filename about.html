About Us

Oxford Pace Intl Ltd is a subsidiary company of Oxford International Group, Nigeria’s number one most diversified commercial business empire with interest in various sectors of the economy such as: Real Estate, Constructions, Agriculture, Properties Management, Travel and Tours, Technology and many more
