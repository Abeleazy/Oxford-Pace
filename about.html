About Us

Oxford Pace Kiddies and Teens is a subsidiary company of Oxford Pace Int’l Ltd (Member Company of Oxford International Group).

Oxford Pace Kiddies and Teens is a company that is focused on children's future growth and their investments.

Oxford Pace Kiddies and Teens has product for kids &Teens, in which you can investment in Land or Real Earnings.

Oxford pace Kiddies and Teens is giving every parent and guardian an avenue to insure the future of their kids through our products.

Vision & Mission

Vision

Building foundation for financial Independence towards achieving the highest possible standard of future CEO across the globe through oxford pace kiddies and teens ltd.

We partner with parent/guardian around the world in the fight to end unemployment among our youth. We tend to build a healthy environment for our children, thriving and empowering them to create lasting change in their own lives and communities.
